About CityMetric

By 2050, 70% of the world's population will live in a city. CityMetric explores all the challenges facing the world's cities as they expand to take in another two billion people.

A partnership between the New Statesman and Timetric, CityMetric combines high quality editorial with proprietary data from its own intelligence unit. It offers news, comment and analysis on key topics including architecture, economics, demographics and transport.

Through journalism, data and analysis, CityMetric tells its audience of companies, policy makers and urbanites everything they need to know the places where they work, invest and live.
